Custom Header

when making your custom header , you will need to add the onclick event listener to each of the links that you want pointing to:

  • your home page - onClick='home()'
  • your about page- onClick='about()'
  • your contact page- onClick='contact()'
  • your blog index page- onClick='blog()'
